Who we are

Ingressive for Good exists to equip young Africans in need with tech skills to help them impact and contribute to the development of Africa, socially and economically. Ingressive For Good (I4G) was created to address the glaring inequities that exist within African tech ecosystems.

Micro-Scholarships

We award partial scholarships for tech final year students in need, to the Ivy Leagues of Africa. This financial support also provides a laptop for the student, to aid their education. Every student who receives this scholarship will automatically become a part of the I4G Alumni.

Technical Training

This is a core digital skills training done in partnership with reputable tech trainers like the HNG and Enye program. The goal is to provide resources and hands-on, practical training to help young Africans gain and perfect tech skills to make them job-ready.

History About Us

While building a successful Venture Capital firm, we recognized the inequities in the tech ecosystem and how bridging those inequities could catalyze growth opportunities for the African continent. So, On July 1st, 2020, we launched a not for profit arm, Ingressive For Good (I4G).

Our mission is to increase the earning power of Africans by training 1,000,000 tech talents in Africa and connecting them to 5,000 jobs. We are doing this in three core ways: Micro-scholarships, Technical Training, and Talent Placement.
